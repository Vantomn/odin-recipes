Authentic Pho

Pho is a delicious and hearty Vietnamese rice noodle soup that is made with beef soup bones, beef, rice noodles, onions, fish sauce, various spices and seasonings like ginger, star anise, and salt, and garnishes like cilantro and green onions. Overall, Pho is quite simple to make but it takes a long time to create the broth.

Ingredients

  • Beef Soup Bones
  • Rice Noodles
  • Beef Top Sirloin
  • Fish Sauce
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Star Anise
  • Salt
  • Cilantro
  • Green Onions

How To Make Pho:

  1. Roast Bones and Onions

    2. Roast the bones and beef until they are brown.

  2. Make the Broth

    3. Combine Roasted beef bones, roasted onions, ginger, salt, star anise, fish sauce water in a large stockpot and allow it to simmer for 6 to 10 hours.

  3. Soak the Rice Noodles

    4. Soak the rice noodles in room temperature water for about an hour.

  4. Cook the Rice Noodles

    5. Once the broth is done, cook the rice noodles in boiling water, drain and add the rice noodles to your bowl topped with sirloin and your favorite garnishes.
